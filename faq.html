MovieTime - FAQ

What is this app?

With the MovieTime application you can look for popular, top rated or upcoming movies in theater and you can save the movies that you would like to see in your watchlist. Once you have seen a movie you can add it to your viewed section.

How do I operate this spaceship of an app?

You first need to create an account under the login page, then you go to the home page and select your movies.

Where have did I get the data and logo from?

MovieTime got the API from https://www.themoviedb.org, and the logo comes from https://www.flaticon.com/authors/freepik
